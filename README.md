# molecule_simulation
2d simulation of atoms interacting
**this is made to look cool but also kind of realistic(not really)**</br>
includes 10 elements and 3 bonds types
### module required
* numbers
* pygame
* subprocess
## Controls
* left click to place a single atom
* right click to change element
* middle click to select an atom
* *0* to s
* *backspace* to clear all "matter"
* *delete* to remove all the heat wave
* *f1* to change the ideal framrate(better framrate or more accurate simulation?)
* *h* to place heat wave
* *up and down* to increase or decrease the temperature
* *f9* to hide the stat
* *PRINTSCREEN* to dump the simulation list into the console
* *f2* to toggle freze
  for when something is selected:
*  *=* to remove electron
*  *-* to add electron
*  *end* to delete the selected atom
*  *t and hold* to move the atom around
*  *escape* to unselect
